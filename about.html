About Me

I am Gary Cheung, a designer based in New York City. Currently, I am working in a marketing & communications role at Excelsior Medical, a large, multispecialty medical group, where I provide in-house visual design services and coordinate company-wide marketing projects. In the past, I designed websites for the University of Michigan's Auxiliary Marketing team and data visualizations as an intern at Arkadium.

As a designer, I approach UX from a cross-disciplinary perspective. My experience working on different design teams, combined with my education background in human-computer interaction and psychology, help me see a design problem from multiple angles.

Proficient in UX Design, User Research, and Visual Design.
